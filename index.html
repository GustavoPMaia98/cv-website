Gustavo Pinho Maia

I am a PhD student and researcher at Centro de Química Estrutural (CQE), collaborating with the Muséum National d’Histoire Naturelle (MNHN) and NASA Goddard. My research sits at the interface of prebiotic chemistry, mechanochemistry and meteoritics, exploring how mechanical energy influences the synthesis and fate of organic molecules relevant to origins of life. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
