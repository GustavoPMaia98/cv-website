Gustavo Pinho Maia

PhD student in Chemistry (Astrobiology) — Centro de Química Estrutural, Universidade de Lisboa

My research explores how mechanical energy influences the synthesis and fate of organic molecules relevant to the origins of life, combining mechanochemistry, meteoritics and prebiotic chemistry in collaborations with MNHN and NASA Goddard.

Astrobiology Mechanochemistry Meteoritics Prebiotic Chemistry
Read full CV Publications Contact

Selected highlights

Mechanochemical reactivity of ribonucleosides, shock-induced degradation of nucleosides, and a PhD funded project by FCT on mechanochemical prebiotic synthesis.
