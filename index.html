Gustavo Pinho Maia

PhD student in Chemistry (Astrobiology) — Centro de Química Estrutural, University of Lisbon

I study how mechanical and shock energies drive the synthesis and transformation of organic molecules relevant to the origins of life. My work combines mechanochemistry, meteoritics and prebiotic chemistry in collaborations with MNHN and NASA Goddard.
