About

Gustavo Pinho Maia completed a BSc in Industrial Chemistry at the University of Beira Interior (2020) and an MSc in Chemistry at Instituto Superior Técnico (2022). He is currently a PhD candidate (Astrobiology) at Universidade de Lisboa, Instituto Superior Técnico, working on mechanochemical energy and prebiotic synthesis.
